What's included with Power flushing?

Power flushing is a deep clean of your central heating system. It pushes cleaning chemicals through the radiators and pipework to help remove sludge, dirty water and debris that can build up inside the system over time.

It can help improve circulation, make radiators heat more evenly and reduce strain on the boiler. If a dirty system is left untreated, radiators may stay cold in places, the boiler can become noisy or less efficient, and sludge can block boiler components, causing the boiler to fault or stop working properly after the installation.
