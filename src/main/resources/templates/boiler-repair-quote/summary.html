What's included in my repair visit?

For £79, one of our experienced Gas Safe engineers will attend your home, inspect the boiler and identify what is causing the issue with your heating or hot water.

If the fault can be resolved on the spot and no replacement parts are needed, we will complete that repair within your £79 visit at no extra labour charge. A large number of straightforward boiler faults fall into this category.

If the job needs replacement parts or additional work, we will explain the issue clearly and provide a fixed repair quote before anything extra goes ahead. Where parts are available locally, we will always aim to complete the work as quickly as possible.

All completed work is checked against our internal standards, and you will know exactly what has been done before the job is signed off.

Some boiler problems are minor and may be easy to resolve yourself, so it is always worth checking our boiler repair guidance and videos before booking if you are unsure.
